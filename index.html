Adalah Council, Inc.

Serving the Northern Virginia (DMV) community through scholarship, guidance, and public education.

Adalah Council, Inc. is a nonprofit organization advancing charitable, religious, and educational purposes through principled Islamic scholarship, research, and public education, addressing contemporary ethical, legal, and social issues in service of the broader public.

Programs & Activities

Adalah Council, Inc. engages in community education, research, and public programming addressing contemporary ethical, legal, and social issues. This includes lectures, publications, and advisory initiatives serving individuals, families, and institutions.
