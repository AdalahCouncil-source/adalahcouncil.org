About Adalah Council

Adalah Council, Inc. is a nonprofit organization established to advance charitable, religious, and educational initiatives through principled Islamic scholarship, research, and public engagement.

The organization seeks to address contemporary ethical, legal, and social issues through scholarship rooted in the Islamic intellectual tradition while remaining attentive to the needs of individuals, families, and communities in the modern world.

Adalah Council supports educational programming, publications, advisory initiatives, and public discourse intended to strengthen access to thoughtful and principled guidance.

Areas of Focus

  • Community education
  • Research and publications
  • Public lectures and programming
  • Family and ethical guidance
  • Institutional and community advisory initiatives
